neurOrange

Orange Picker

A gentle mini-lab showing how your visual system adapts and reveals complementary color. Pick an orange, fixate on its opposite for ~15 seconds, then look at a white screen to see a brief “ghost” orange made by perception.

SAFETY & LIMITS
  • Use a dim room
  • Moderate your screen brightness
  • Rest between trials
Above is a sped up simulation of the effects of the illusion.
Press Start to create your own!
How it works: prolonged viewing adapts neurons tuned to the opposite of orange (blue/cyan). When you switch to white, the opponent pathway briefly leads, so perception leans toward orange—this is called opponent-process adaptation.
Optimized for iPhone portrait orientation.
